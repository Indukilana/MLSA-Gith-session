ROOMS

Life is rosy when you stay in a charming room overlooking the relaxing oceanic view. Wander on the beach and bathe under the sun and stay in our luxurious rooms with unmatched comfort. Featuring warm, rich decor our rooms are perfect for unwinding all the busy days you had so far. Choose from our wide array of lavish rooms and enjoy a whole different view of magnificent ocean right from your private balcony. Ideal for a laidback family holiday. Make yourself at home and enjoy the glittering views with us.

POOL

After a long day of traveling or sightseeing, there’s nothing like a refreshing dip in the pool. If you’re looking to make a splash on your next vacation, relax at our luxurious indoor pool. With gorgeous designs and indulgent features, your afternoon swim will be taken to another level. Enjoy the timeless aesthetic while taking a refreshing dip in the pool or bring your kids to play on the Splash Pad!

GARDEN

Our garden seemlessly blend with the pool alongside to create a restful and peaceful scenery. You can enjoy a perfect morning dip in the pool and lounge at out exquisite garden. From fragrant flowers to well manicured green carpet like lawns, our garden is truly exceptional. Take home some beautiful photographs of your loved ones amidst the scenic beauty we've been able to create with our garden to look and cherish the remarkable memories you had during your stay at our hotel.

OUTDOOR PLAY AREA

Children are never bored at Sea Side South Park Hotel! Here the children can be more adventurous in a safe environment and work off some of their energy in the fresh sea air. It offers old favourites like swings and climbing frames and also new attractions like the stand on see-saw. There is a separate area for the smaller children containing three sit on rides that bounce and sway keeping even the wee ones amused. Adults can relax on park benches and enjoy watching the kids having fun.

FOOD & BEVERAGES

Eat to your heart’s content with our extensive menu including lavishingly fresh gourmet seafood. Spoil yourself with some culinary pampering moments and relaxed environment. Enjoy delicacies from the extensive buffet. If privacy is what you are looking for, enjoy our specialty dining arrangements or order delicacies through room service. When you are here, we highly recommend the multi-cuisine options where creativity, taste and freshness comes alive exclusively hand crafted by our team of Chef’s.

MUSICAL EVENINGS

To ring in your evening with some musical flair, we're proud to host some of Sri Lanka’s finest musicians and performers for a musical evening encompassing everything from genres such as classical music to light rock and jazz. Enjoy the live, soothing and high quality music in such an intimate setting engulfed in the serene sea breeze with a variety of evening beverages and snacks of your choice.

EVENING DANCING EVENTS

Lift up your typical evening a notch higher. Come Dancing with your family and friends! Our banquet hall is all ready. Sea Side South Park Hotel has everything you need for the perfect evening with a little dance. Featuring dazzling lights, toe tapping music and exquisite cuisine to satiate an array of tastebuds, all of which adds up to an unforgettable experience. Add a little groove to your evening and share a lovely time with your loved ones.
