ABOUT



The Sea Side South Park Hotel has everything you could possibly wish for in a tropical beach resort, and much more. There’s something to suit everyone, whether you’re visiting for business or pleasure, relaxation or stimulation.

We offer comfortably equipped rooms, with panoramic views of the ocean directly from your private balcony, beautiful golden sandy beaches just outside, multi-cuisine restaurant, fully stocked bar, cocktails, sun deck facilities as well. Transform your everyday activities into extraordinary experiences, from beachfront breakfast to relaxing poolside or indulging in a signature spa treatment.

Soak yourself in balmy tropical waves and get glowing suntan this vacation. Enjoy lavish gourmet seafood and stroll on the beach while we take care of everything. Just relax and enjoy at an unique luxury retreat with indulging amenities.

FESTIVALS



A sky full of kites - Annually in mid of the August

Have an exclusive experience of The Kite Festival where sky comes alive with thousands of flying shapes of every shade. This is open to all. People from all age groups and backgrounds can display their creativity. The competition covers the longest kite, most innovative kite, kites made of natural resources and many more. The atmosphere will be covered in great sense of excitement. You can purchase kites from vendors and join right in. The Kite Festival is absolutely a day full of wonders.

Unforgettable experience of nightlife - Every weekend

If you're looking out for a vibrant party atmosphere head straight to us. We host variety of beach parties throughout the year. The beach front has a promenade of the restaurant and the bar filled with lively crowd grooving to loud music. The cocktails are served at a reasonable price with warm smiles from our guest-friendly staff. Party goers can lounge and peacefully unwind with family and friends. Do not miss the party at this venue of a sun-soaked beach during the stunning sunset by the dusk.

The beat goes on - Last Saturday of every month

The Outdoor Beach Concert is the perfect complement to a wonderful beach vacation. This event has emerged as a hotspot for live entertainment with family and friends - offering the best in music and celebrity concerts. Groove to the live music from dawn to dusk. Experience the full spectrum with music to your ears and beat to your feet.


Colour me happy - Annually in March

The holi festival - festival of colours is day worth rejoicing.The vibrancy of colours is something that brings in a lot of positivity in our lives.The golden shores of the beach will be canvassed with an array of bright colours as we host the holi festival to pay homage to symbolic triumph of good over evil. Celebrate it in a modern twist with music, dance and lots of colours! Our unique style of holi celebration at the beach is definitely going to be an unforgettable experience that you don't want to miss.
